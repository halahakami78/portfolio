Biography

BIOGRAPHY

Hala Hakami is a programmer and artist from Jeddah, Saudi Arabia. Blending a passion for technology with a love of creativity, she moves between two worlds — the structured logic of programming and the expressive freedom of art. Her interests span impression art, photography, and a wide range of hobbies that allow her to experiment and grow through different forms of expression. Her gallery reflects this duality, showcasing pieces that capture both technical precision and artistic imagination. Whether through code, brushstrokes, or a camera lens, Hala’s work carries a sense of curiosity and meaning, inviting others to see familiar ideas in new ways. She continues to explore the balance between logic and emotion, creating projects that highlight her dedication to learning, creativity, and self-expression.
