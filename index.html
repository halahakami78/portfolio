ABOUT

I’m Hala Hakami, a programmer and artist from Jeddah, Saudi Arabia. My journey has always been shaped by curiosity and creativity — whether through writing code, painting impression pieces, or capturing moments through photography. I find inspiration in both structure and spontaneity, moving fluidly between the logical world of programming and the expressive freedom of art. Art and technology, for me, are not opposites but companions. Programming sharpens my problem-solving skills, while art allows me to explore emotion and perspective. My gallery highlights this balance, showcasing a love for impression art, photography, and the hobbies that fuel my imagination. In everything I do, I aim to create work that is thoughtful, expressive, and meaningful — something that reflects who I am while leaving room for others to find their own stories within it.
